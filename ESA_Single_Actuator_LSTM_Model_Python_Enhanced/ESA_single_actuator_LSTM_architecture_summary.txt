Enhanced Single Actuator LSTM Architecture Summary
===============================================

Conceptual layer structure:
1. Sequence input layer with 1 feature: current
2. LSTM recurrent layer 1 with 64 hidden units
3. LSTM recurrent layer 2 with 64 hidden units
4. Fully connected hidden layer with 32 units
5. ReLU activation layer
6. Fully connected output layer with 2 outputs

Why two LSTM layers are used:
- The first recurrent layer learns short term temporal behavior and local memory effects.
- The second recurrent layer refines higher level nonlinear dynamic patterns.
- A single LSTM layer may be too limited when the actuator response contains delays, transient memory, and noisy behavior.
- More than two recurrent layers would likely be unnecessary for this small dataset and could increase overfitting risk.

Sequence length: 25
Training ratio: 0.7
Validation ratio: 0.15
Smoothing used: True
Smoothing window: 5
